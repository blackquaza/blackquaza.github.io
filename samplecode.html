This is a neural network I wrote in Python to detect credit card fraud. The data used is anonymised and is fed into a two-layer perceptron. 800 valid transaction and 100 fraudulent transactions are loaded into the program, and weights are discovered. Then, another set of transactions is tested and the results are shown.
